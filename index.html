Our Story

Stimuli Network is an emerging initiative focused on empowering individuals to achieve their personal and professional goals. Founded by Adrian Mowrey, a Computer Science undergraduate at the University of the People #UoPeople, our core belief is that everyone deserves the opportunity to reach their full potential.

We aim to build a supportive network offering crucial resources. We are actively seeking partners and funding to launch this mission and benefit all who need support.

Adrian Mowrey, Founder
